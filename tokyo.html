Tokyo Ramen(ときょ)

When I was reaserching different types of ramen i found it quite interesting that one was named after a city. Now I think about it it actuay makes sense since it was created in Tokyo.

Tokyo ramen is a traditional japanese noodle soup with a soy type broth it contains springy noodles and various toppings such as seaweed, braised pork and seasoned bamboo shoots!
